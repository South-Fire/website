About Emily and Matias

Emily Davies brings a fiery passion to South Fire, drawing from her roots in Southern barbecue traditions. Growing up in a family that cherished slow-cooked meals and backyard gatherings, she honed her craft through years of experimenting with open flames, blending bold spices with time-honored techniques to create dishes that feel like home.

Matias Sallaberry, with his Argentine heritage, infuses South Fire with the soul of asado. His love for fire-cooking was sparked as a kid, watching his abuelo grill over glowing embers. Matias crafts each dish with precision and heart, turning every event into a celebration of flavor and connection.

Dining Experience

Our approach to cooking is rooted in tradition, blending traditional South American fire-cooking techniques with the best organic and local produce from our partnered farms or our garden. Each menu is crafted to reflect the season, the occasion, and the people around the fire.

Seated Multi-Course Meal

A refined tasting menu showcasing the depth and elegance of fire-cooked cuisine.

Family-Style Feast

A convivial dining experience with generous platters for sharing, emphasizing warmth and connection.

Live Fire Experience

An intimate, hands-on culinary journey for small groups looking to connect with the process, featuring guided fire-cooking techniques and a meal prepared entirely over open flames.
