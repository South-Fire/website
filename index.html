Our Story

SOUTH FIRE brings the unique flavors and traditions of South America to life through captivating hospitality events and authentic food products. We source our ingredients locally and sustainably, and use the artistic skill of cooking with fire and fermentation to create exceptional culinary experiences. Our goal is to provide our customers with an authentic and memorable taste of South America. We are proud to be in partnership with Zambri's Restaurant to make it all happen!

The Experience

Private Dinners

Picture this—a cozy evening under the stars, the crackle of the fire, and the mouthwatering smell of slow-cooked meats. We'll create a menu just for you, with dishes that tell a story and a setting that feels like home.

Festivals

Get ready for a feast for the senses! Our festivals are a celebration of fire, food, and community. Watch as our chefs work their magic over open flames, and join in the fun with music, dancing, and plenty of laughter.

Collaborations

We love teaming up with local talents to create something special. Whether it's a pop-up dinner with a guest chef or a workshop on fire-cooking techniques, these events are all about sharing knowledge and passion.
