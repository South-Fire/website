Our Story

SOUTH FIRE brings the unique flavors and traditions of South America to life through captivating hospitality events and authentic food products. We source our ingredients locally and sustainably, and use the artistic skill of cooking with fire and fermentation to create exceptional culinary experiences. Our goal is to provide our customers with an authentic and memorable taste of South America.

We are proud to be in partnership with Zambri's Restaurant.

The Experience

Private Dinners

Festivals
